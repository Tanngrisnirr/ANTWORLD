About AntWorld

AntWorld.org is dedicated to making ant identification accessible to researchers, students, and enthusiasts. Our interactive dichotomous keys guide you through morphological features to identify ant species found across the Palearctic region.

All data is freely available for scientific and educational purposes under Creative Commons license.
