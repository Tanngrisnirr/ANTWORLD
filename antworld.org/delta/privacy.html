AntWorld.org is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Do NOT Do

  • Sell, rent, or monetize any user data
  • Use third-party tracking or analytics services
  • Require account creation or personal information

What We Do Collect

For basic site functionality and improvement, we collect:

  • IP addresses - For security and aggregate statistics
  • Page visit counts - To understand which content is useful
  • Session duration - Approximate time spent per visit
  • Referrer data - Where visitors come from
  • Browser cache -

Local Storage

We use browser local storage only for:

  • Theme preference - Light or dark mode setting
  • Language preference - Your chosen language

This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.
